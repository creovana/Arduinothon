About Arduinothon 2.0

ARDUINOTHON-2.0 is a prestigious state-level competition organized by the Electronics and Telecommunication Department at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, Tumakuru. Taking place on April 23, 2025, this event challenges participants to conceptualize, design, and build an innovative Micro-Controller-based project within 8 hours—completely on the spot. With no prior preparation, students must leverage their technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and creativity under intense time constraints to develop functional prototypes using microcontrollers, sensors, and electronic components. The event fosters rapid innovation, teamwork, and hands-on hardware experience, providing a competitive yet collaborative platform for students to showcase their engineering and programming skills.

Rules and Guidelines

  • Participants must register in teams of 2-4 members.
  • All projects must be Micro-Controller-based and include functional hardware elements.
  • Participants are responsible for their own components and materials.
  • No prior preparation is allowed; everything must be built during the competition.
  • Organizers and volunteers are not liable for any loss or damage to personal equipment.
  • Projects must be completed within the 8-hour competition window.
  • All participants must adhere to the event schedule and guidelines provided by the organizers.
